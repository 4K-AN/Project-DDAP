About NgeMEAL

Founded in 2023, NgeMEAL was created by a group of food-loving software developers who were tired of unhealthy takeout options during long coding sessions. We understand the unique challenges of maintaining a healthy diet while meeting tight deadlines and solving complex problems.

Our menu is designed with both nutrition and flavor in mind. We source local, fresh ingredients and prepare dishes that provide balanced macronutrients to keep your brain sharp and your energy levels stable throughout the day.

Whether you're a solo developer working from home, or part of a large tech team in an office, NgeMEAL has flexible options to fit your schedule and dietary preferences. Our goal is simple: to fuel great code with great food.
